The Target Audience Description for Forecast Ninja:

The intended visitors are the general public living in Eastern Idaho

  • Age: 18-100
  • Gender: Male/Female
  • Location: Eastern Idaho
  • Language: American English
  • Education: Any
  • Income: $0 - 80,0000
