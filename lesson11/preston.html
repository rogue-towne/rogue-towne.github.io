Founders Day Fresco

Families get your picnic baskets ready because the greastest celebration of the year of Preston is around the corner. Some pople look forward to this every year and we want to make sure everyone is excited. This year we have set up a stage for local bands and artists from Idaho to come and play during the festivities. We have carnival rides, clowns, a petting zoo and most importantly food trucks galore! Hosting a wide variety of options is the national association of fodd trucks that are visiting Preston for this weekend! We have designated picnic areas with tables and open grass for you to grab your blankets. From italian to Greek and even authentic Indain Fry bread, we're planning everyone. Games and water activities like slip and slide for the kids and cornhole and horsehoes for the adults, the Fresco is here and we're excited to see you there!
