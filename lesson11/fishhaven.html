The Fish Are Biting

"It wasn't hard to get a pole but it was hard to catch a fish." -Fish Haven Local. The past year somwthing was in the water and the temperatures were off. There was a problem with the fish and people weren't having success. "I would talk to my friends and they were trying everything. Worms, goo, pizza, crickets, bread, whatever they could think of. But the fish just weren't biting. Some of us evern joked that the fish got Covid too!" -Sam Joe, local High School Fishing Addict. After so much time and fears that the fish were disappearing due to weather conditions, suprisingly over the last month there has been a major influx of fish being caught and fishers are having a lot of succes in the lake. The fish are biting, so come to the lake for an afternoon and go fishing!
