Upcoming Events:

Farmer's Market

The annual Farmer's Market in August is coming up and produce will be sold by local sellers who will be joining from around the community. For 60 years this market has been going on and is a tradition in Soda Springs that has lived on despite past droughts. The community has always stepped up and supported the local growers and we want invite the whole community to join in this year. You can buy world-famous Idaho Potatoes!! After dealing with COVID-19 this last year, there were worries that the event couldn't take place as it attracts community members from all across Idaho but we're so happy to announce that the event will go on as planned and no masks will be required due to the small amount of current cases. We will be posting shortly the sellers and more specifically what products are expeected to be sold shortly. Obviously a wide choice of produce, organically grown will be sold along with local delicacies from small local businesses. A list of businesses will be announced shortly so check back soon to find out who will be there!
